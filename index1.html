Who We Are
GRF is a NITI Aayog-recognized Section 8 organization specializing in high-impact CSR initiatives. We bridge the gap between corporate vision and community transformation by designing scalable programs in healthcare, education, and sustainability — fully aligned with Section 135 Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013.
Leveraging a multi-disciplinary team of legal, financial, and CSR experts, GRF transforms complex mandates into transparent, third-party audited results. Through our elite Advisory Board and robust corporate network, we ensure every project is compliant, accountable, and engineered for deep social and financial impact.
